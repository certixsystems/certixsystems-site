Variance Narrative

The primary variance driver for Q1 FFY 2026 is FMAP misalignment within HCBS Waiver Services, accounting for $42.8M of the total $81.6M gross variance. Enhanced FMAP rates authorized under Section 9817 of the American Rescue Plan Act were not applied to three waiver subcategories, resulting in underreported federal share.

Secondary contributors include a $31.4M prior-period adjustment carry-forward from Q4 FFY 2025 (Inpatient Hospital) and a $18.6M category reclassification in Prescribed Drugs. A $11.2M reporting lag offset in Outpatient/Clinic partially reduces net variance exposure.

Recommended Corrective Actions
  • Apply corrected FMAP rates to HCBS waiver subcategories 1915(c), 1915(i), and 1915(k)
  • Reconcile Q4 FFY 2025 inpatient carry-forward before next submission
  • Reclassify specialty pharmacy expenditures to Prescribed Drugs (Line 5)
